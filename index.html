About

Our college got registered as a CSI-Student Chapter in the year 2006. It comes under the Region 5 of CSI. At present we have many of our faculties as life members and more than 500 student members. CSI coordinator of our college is Dr. T.AdiLakshmi, CSE HOD. CSI student branch counsellor is Mrs. Anitha.
